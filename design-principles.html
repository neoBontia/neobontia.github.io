Visual Heirarchy

Dungeons & Dragons

https://dnd.wizards.com/

Visual Heirarchy was used on this page to showcase Dungeons & Dragons' new handbook. Displaying the cover page of the manual attracts the eyes of its viewers and the large font that displays the manual's name solidifies the attention of the viewer. The 'Learn More' button then enticies the viewer to interact with it.

White Space and Clean Design

Calendly

https://calendly.com/

Calendly's website design is so simple that looks clean and user-friendly. Calendly would want to sell their product as a professional yet easy-to-use scheduling application, and the way its site is design sure sells their product that way.

Hick's Law

GrabFood

https://food.grab.com/ph/en/

Before choosing which restaurant to order food from, it is easier to know which food you would want to eat. There are a plethora of varieties of food and that makes it usually hard to choose what you would like to it. By adding a filter system of which food one would like to eat, it becomes less work for the user to choose.
