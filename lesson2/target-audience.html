Weather with us targets those that are dependant on the weather. These may be those that work outside, those that plan to travel, those that are planning activities outside, or could even be for those that want to study the weather. However, based on the website design, Weather with us only accomodates those that lives within Preston, Soda Springs, or Fish Haven, Idaho. The website is also designed to accomodate mobile and desktop view.

Persona 1

Name:

Ronan Hill

Age:

22

Occupation:

Student

Scenarios:

  • Ronan's usual mode of transportation to school, his car, has a flat tire. He has a bike that he can ride to go to school but he needs to know if it will not rain today. If it will rain however, he will just take the bus.
  • Ronan likes to play basketball with his friends. He wants to invite them over to his church to play there three days from now. He wants to know how the weather would be three days from now.

Persona 2

Name:

Jhobell Tapang

Age:

31

Teacher

Scenarios:

  • It has been raining for a whole day now and Jhobell is worried if a storm is coming so that she can advise her class if classes would be suspended because of the storm.

  • Jhobell plans to buy groceries for the whole week. She plans to cook hot dishes on rainy days so that her children will not feel so cold during the rain.
